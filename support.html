Frequently asked

  • How do I unlock Pro? Open the Pro card on the Home screen (or tap Transcribe / Rehearse on a question). You can buy a one-time lifetime unlock or a monthly subscription.
  • I bought Pro on another device. Tap “Restore Purchases” on the paywall.
  • Where are my recordings stored? Everything stays on your device — nothing is uploaded. See our Privacy Policy.
  • How do I cancel a subscription? iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Take Cue.

常见问题

  • 如何解锁 Pro？ 在首页点 Pro 卡片（或在任意问题上点 Transcribe / Rehearse）。可选择一次性永久买断，或按月订阅。
  • 在别的设备买过 Pro？ 在订阅页点「Restore Purchases（恢复购买）」。
  • 录像存在哪里？ 全部仅保存在你的设备本机，不会上传。详见 隐私政策。
  • 如何取消订阅？ iPhone 设置 → 你的名字 → 订阅 → Take Cue。

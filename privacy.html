Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

Short version: Your recordings, transcripts, questions, and settings stay on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no ads, and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

Take Cue (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an interview-practice app that lets you record, review, and re-take answers to your own interview questions. This policy explains exactly what happens to your information.

1. Information stored on your device

Everything you create in Take Cue is saved locally on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Video and audio recordings of your practice answers.
  • Interview questions and details you type, paste, or scan.
  • Transcripts generated from your recordings.
  • App settings and progress (e.g. recording layout, reminders, practice counts).

We do not have access to any of this. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

2. Camera and microphone

The app uses your camera and microphone solely to record your practice answers on the device. Recordings are saved locally for you to review and are never uploaded to us.

3. Speech recognition (transcription)

If you use the transcription feature, Take Cue uses Apple’s Speech Recognition to convert your recorded answer into text. Depending on your device and language, Apple may process the audio on-device or send it to Apple’s servers to perform the recognition. This processing is performed by Apple and is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. Take Cue does not receive or store this audio on any server of ours; the resulting transcript is saved only on your device.

4. Photo Library

If you choose to export a recording, the app saves the video to your Photo Library. The app only adds the items you export; it does not read or access your existing photos.

5. Notifications

If you enable practice reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device using a countdown to your interview date. These reminders are generated entirely on-device; no reminder data is sent anywhere.

6. Purchases (Take Cue Pro)

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We never see or receive your payment details. We only receive an anonymous indication from Apple of whether your Pro subscription is currently active, in order to unlock Pro features.

7. What we do NOT do

  • We do not operate any servers that store your content.
  • We do not use accounts or logins.
  • We do not include advertising.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.

8. Children’s privacy

Take Cue does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and stores all content locally on the device.

9. Your control over your data

You can delete individual recordings and interviews inside the app at any time, and deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the “Effective date” above and post the new version at this URL.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: lei.dingfei@gmail.com

隐私政策（中文）

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 3 日

一句话版本：你的录像、文字稿、问题和设置全部保存在你的设备本机。我们没有服务器、不需要账号、没有广告，也没有任何分析或追踪。我们不收集、不出售、不分享你的个人数据。

1. 保存在你设备上的信息

你在本应用中创建的一切都仅保存在本机、不会上传给我们：练习录像（含音频）、你输入/粘贴/扫描的面试问题与详情、由录像生成的文字稿、应用设置与进度。删除应用即从设备移除这些数据。

2. 摄像头与麦克风

仅用于在本机录制你的练习回答。录像保存在本机供你回看，绝不会上传给我们。

3. 语音识别（转写）

若你使用转写功能，本应用调用 Apple 语音识别将录音转为文字。视设备与语言而定，Apple 可能在设备本机处理，或将音频发送至 Apple 服务器进行识别——该处理由 Apple 执行，受 Apple 隐私政策 约束。本应用不在我们的任何服务器上接收或存储该音频，生成的文字稿仅保存在你的设备本机。

4. 相册

若你选择导出录像，本应用会将视频存入你的相册。应用只写入你导出的内容，不会读取你已有的照片。

5. 通知

若你开启练习提醒，应用会在本机按面试倒计时安排本地通知。提醒完全在本机生成，不向任何地方发送。

6. 订阅付费（Take Cue Pro）

订阅由 Apple 通过 App Store（StoreKit）处理。我们看不到、也不接收你的支付信息，仅从 Apple 处获得「Pro 订阅当前是否有效」这一匿名状态用于解锁功能。

7. 我们不会做的事

不运营存储你内容的服务器；不使用账号或登录；无广告；不使用分析、崩溃上报或第三方追踪 SDK；不跨应用/网站追踪你；不向任何人出售或分享你的数据。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用不会故意收集任何人（含儿童）的个人信息，分级为 4+，所有内容均存于设备本机。

9. 你对数据的控制

你可随时在应用内删除单条录像与面试；删除应用即清除设备上所有相关数据。

10. 政策变更

如有变更，我们会更新上方「生效日期」并在本网址发布新版本。
